If at any time you wish to delete your account, please contact us at hello@smartsail.co.uk. You will be asked to provide your email, name, and other details to establish that you own the account before it is deleted.

All data, including your account login, any boats and sails that you own and all records relating to sail usage and location data associated race recordings will be deleted.

Smart Sail retains database backups of all data for a period of 1 month, your data will remain on our backup storage for a month, after which it will automtically be deleted.
